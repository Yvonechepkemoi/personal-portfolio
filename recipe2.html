Beef Stir Fry

Home About Uji wa Wimbi Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g beef strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add beef and cook until browned.
  3. Add onions and bell pepper.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and cook for 5 mins.
  5. Serve hot with rice or chapati.
