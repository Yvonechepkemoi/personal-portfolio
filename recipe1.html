Uji wa Wimbi

Home About Uji wa Wimbi Beef Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup wimbi flour
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp sugar or honey
  • Optional: milk or lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix flour in a little cold water to make a paste.
  2. Boil the rest of the water in a sufuria.
  3. Add the paste and stir constantly.
  4. Simmer for 10 minutes until thickened.
  5. Add sugar or honey, serve hot!
